Who We Are

We are a Prophetic Ministry lead by Rev. Peter Thiong'o in Zimmerman Nairobi. Our doors are open to everyone seeking to grow in their relationship with God.

On this Ministry, we will help you to grow in Christ by spreading the Gospel and building faith in God through the seeds of God's Word. Let's spread the Gospel of God Together, Shalom!!

Join us for worship services, Bible study, and fellowship as we grow together in faith and love.
