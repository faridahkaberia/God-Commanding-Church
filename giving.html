Give

Your generosity helps us fulfill our mission and serve our community

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

- 2 Corinthians 9:7 (NIV)

The Heart of Giving

Giving is an act of worship and obedience to God. When we give, we acknowledge that everything we have comes from Him, and we participate in His work here on earth. Your tithes and offerings enable us to spread the Gospel, minister to those in need, support missions, maintain our facilities, and impact our community for Christ.

Through your faithful giving, we are able to conduct weekly services, run our various ministries, support the poor and needy, organize outreach programs, and maintain our church facilities. Every contribution, regardless of size, makes a significant difference in advancing God's kingdom.

We believe that giving should come from a grateful heart, in response to God's abundant blessings in our lives. It is not about the amount you give, but the attitude with which you give. God sees your heart and honors your faithfulness.

Thank You for Your Generosity!

We are deeply grateful for your faithful support and partnership in ministry. Your giving is making an eternal impact, touching lives, and building God's kingdom. May the Lord bless you abundantly as you continue to be a blessing to others.
