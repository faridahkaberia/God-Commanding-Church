Our Story

How We Began

God's Commanding Church was founded in 2012 by Rev. Peter Thiong'o and Pastor Florence Thiong'o, who were called by God to establish a church that would be a beacon of hope and transformation in the community. What started as a small prayer group in their living room has grown into a vibrant congregation of believers committed to spreading the Gospel.

From humble beginnings, the church has witnessed tremendous growth, both spiritually and numerically. Our founders' vision was to create a place where people could encounter God's presence, experience genuine fellowship, and be equipped to live purposeful lives that glorify God.

Over the years, we've seen countless lives transformed, families restored, and communities impacted through our various outreach programs and ministries. What remains constant is our commitment to biblical teaching and our passion for reaching the lost.

What We Believe

At God's Commanding Church, we believe in the authority and inerrancy of the Holy Scriptures as the inspired Word of God. We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose again on the third day, and is now seated at the right hand of God the Father. We believe that salvation is a gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives, empower believers for service, and guide the church in fulfilling the Great Commission. We are committed to worship, prayer, fellowship, discipleship, and service as essential expressions of our faith.

Our mission is to make disciples of all nations, teaching them to observe all that Christ has commanded, and to be His witnesses in Jerusalem, Judea, Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.

Our Ministries

Serving God and building community through various ministries

Youth Ministry

Empowering the next generation to live boldly for Christ. Our youth ministry provides a safe space for young people to grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and discover their purpose in God's kingdom.

Women Ministry

A community of women supporting and encouraging each other in faith and life. Through Bible studies, fellowship, and outreach, we strive to be women after God's own heart, making a difference in our families and communities.

Men Ministry

Building men of integrity and spiritual leadership. Our men's ministry focuses on discipleship, accountability, and equipping men to be godly leaders in their homes, workplaces, and communities.

Sunday School

Nurturing young hearts to love Jesus. Our Sunday School provides age-appropriate biblical teaching in a fun and engaging environment, helping children develop a strong foundation of faith from an early age.
